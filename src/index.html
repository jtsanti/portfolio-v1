A Bit About Me

Thanks for visiting! My name is Justin and I'm a web developer with a passion for front end design.

I've been in the space for a few years now and am always looking for new opportunities to grow myself and my craft. My journey began back in 2020 where my company at the time had me working with WYSIWYG editors to make visual changes through our CMS. I developed an insatiable curiosity as I strived to learn how to do new and cool things with their themes and that sparked the idea that this may be something to explore further. After taking a few courses at a local college, I knew I was hooked and I haven't looked back. While right now I've been working mainly with the front end, I'm working towards going full stack in the future.

When I'm taking breaks from coding or learning, you can usually find me in the kitchen trying my hand at a new recipe or picking up a new board game to try out.
